The Witcher Wild Hunt

Witcher Schools

01.
School of the Wolf

The Order of Witchers had almost completely splintered, only a small group of witchers remained in its keep after the third group of witchers had left to build their own school. These last witchers left Morgraig and travelled east. They decided to settle in the north in the caves of the Morhen Valley.

In the 11th century the construction of Kaer Morhen was finished, and because its original builders apparently abandoned the keep or vanished in some other way, the Wolves settled there.

02.
School of the Cat

School of the Cat is a school of ragtag witchers formed after a student mutiny against their former masters. Headquartered in the traveling Dyn Marv Caravan, they are apparently one of the few schools training women and non-pureblood humans.

Cat students hone their balance by walking a tightrope blindfolded. Failure is easy to shrug off at first, but throughout their training, the walk gets higher and the danger increases.

03.
School of the Griffin

School of the Griffin, sometimes referred to as School of the Eagle or Gryphon School, is a school of witchers headquartered in Kovir and Poviss, in Kaer Seren located at the sea end of a mountain range. Leo Bonhart had reportedly slain at least one of its members.

Students are offered two choices to complete their final trial. They either locate and retrieve a griffin's egg, or recite the entirety of Liber Tenebrarum verbatim. However, none have ever chosen the latter.

04.
School of the Bear

The School of the Bear was a school of witchers headquartered in Haern Caduch keep in Amell, reportedly in the Slopes.

Students must pass the trial of the mountain. This requires venturing to the peak of Mount Gorgon to retrieve a special runestone as proof of ascent. It was an especially brutal challenge as many boys freeze to death long before they find the summit.

05.
School of the Viper

The School of the Viper was a school of witchers headquartered in Gorthur Gvaed, a keep somewhere in the deep chasms of Tir Tochair mountains.

Each student is given a pet to raise during their stay at the keep, they would form a strong emotional bond through their training. Years later, before becoming a fully-fledged witcher, they are ordered to slaughter their animal.

06.
School of the Manticore

The School of the Manticore, or Alqatil (Zerrikanian: Slayers), is a witcher school that was headquartered in the far east, on the edges of the Korath desert. They rarely venture on the other side of the Continent, and thus remain mostly an enigma to Nordlings.
